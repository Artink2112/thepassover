Co-op

 

For this summer we are offering a Co-op placement for interested students. The placement will be an accurate simulation of being a freelance mobile game developer. The students will work from home and remotely report to the assigned project manager and together as a team will build an app, marketing material and website all while acquiring knowledge on the freelance industry and whats its like to work with real world deadlines. The co-op placement can accompany kids of any computer science level, from advanced to novice.

About Us

      905 Games had a humble beginning starting in late august of 2017, when our founder had the idea of a mobile game based around his school. He thought and drew out his idea in late September. Work started on the companies first game "Notebook Ninja", it was a Windows PC based game and took him months to design and co-develop. the game was entered into and displayed by him at  a game making competition at the University of Toronto, where he won 3rd best game in the school board. After the high of creating Notebook Ninja wore off he started to work on the mobile game he had dreamed of back in the summer. He decided to go solo on the development, design and publishing of the game. The mobile game now titled "Goetz Run" is in beta and pending publication on the Google Playstore for all Android based devices to play on. He has talked and shown the game to many in school officials and may become the official game of the school.
